About Us

Nova Arafa Palace

Make Kochi your haven at Nova Arafa Palace! Choose from fully-furnished one-bedroom, two-bedroom, or studio apartments, each featuring open kitchens, comfortable living & dining areas, and private bathrooms with split-AC and water heaters.
Ideal for families, medical visitors (we offer short-term rentals!), business travelers, and everyone in between, our convenient location puts you near Lulu Mall, Wonderla Amusement Park, top healthcare facilities like ASTER Medicity and AMRITA Institute, and even Cochin International Airport.
Experience affordable comfort and explore Kochi's vibrant soul from your home away from home. Whether you're staying daily or monthly, Nova Arafa Palace is your perfect Kochi haven.
